About Us

Little Lemon, a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, is located in the heart of Chicago, Illinois. They offer a unique blend of traditional recipes with a contemporary flair.

The culinary team at Little Lemon, inspired by Italian, Greek, and Turkish cultures, curates a rotating menu of 12-15 items each season.

With its rustic charm and relaxed ambiance, Little Lemon offers moderately priced dishes, making it a favorite dining spot at any time of the day.

Little Lemon is the brainchild of two Italian siblings, Mario and Adrian, who immigrated to the United States with the collective dream of establishing their own restaurant.

Mario, one of the owners, utilizes cherished family recipes and his culinary expertise from Italy to create Little Lemon's menu.

Adrian, the other owner, handles all the marketing for Little Lemon and spearheaded the initiative to diversify the menu, incorporating more than just traditional Italian dishes, but also other Mediterranean cuisines.
